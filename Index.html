Windows Service Restart

A console driven app, designed to check the status of a specified Windows Service and Restart/Start accordingly.

Technologies

  • System.Diagnostics.EventLog | Version: 7.0.0
  • System.Security.Principal.Windows | Version: 5.0.0
  • System.ServiceProcess.ServiceController | Version: 7.0.1

Functionality

  • IdentityDBContext Integraton
  • ASP.NET Identity
  • AI-Based Text Generation
  • Verse Of The Day
  • Text Reminder (Email/Phone)
